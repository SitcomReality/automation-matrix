# Visual Overhaul: Roadmap & Progress

Tracking the factory's evolution into a "Blue Ball Machine" / "Automation Matrix" aesthetic.

## Completed Visual Enhancements
- **The Core 3x3 Suite**: All major machines (Polisher, Blender, Prism, Weaver, Apotheosis, Sacrifice) have unique, multi-stage mechanical animations.
- **Logistics Overhaul**: Miners have drilling animations; Belts feature moving chevrons; Splitters have a central logic-core.
- **Atmospheric Effects**: Implemented Global Particle Pool (sparks, smoke), Screen Shake for heavy cycles, and rhythmic Steam Vents.
- **Archimedes Screw**: (Just Added) A 3x1 transport machine for high-speed item delivery with a rotating spiral thread.

## Future Whimsical Ideas
- **Pendulum Sorter**: A 2x1 rhythmic arm that "throws" items between adjacent belts instead of standard sliding.
- **Item Cannon**: A 1x4 launcher that fires items through the air to a 2x2 catch-net.
- **Fluidic Manifold**: Decorative pipes that connect nearby machines, pulsing with the same hue as the items being processed.
- **Jumping Belts**: Short-range 1x1 pads that bounce items over other belts.
